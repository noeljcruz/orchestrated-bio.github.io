Orchestrated Biosciences ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website orchestrated.bio and use our services.

Automatically collected information: We may collect standard web analytics data including IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This data is collected through standard server logs and analytics tools.

We use collected information to:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our website uses third-party services for analytics and content delivery. These services have their own privacy policies. We use:

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics to distinguish unique users and track sessions. These are analytics cookies, not advertising cookies. We do not use cross-site tracking or advertising cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by declining cookies when prompted on our site.
