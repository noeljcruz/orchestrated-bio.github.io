AI Radar

Competitive Intelligence Automation

AI Radar continuously monitors academic preprints, research databases, AI model hubs, open-source repos, and industry news to track competitors, market shifts, and emerging threats. Every finding is AI-scored for relevance, categorized by impact, and synthesized into weekly strategic briefings — replacing hours of manual research with an autonomous intelligence pipeline that never sleeps.

Key Features

7-Source Monitoring

Scans arXiv, bioRxiv/medRxiv, Semantic Scholar, Hugging Face, GitHub, RSS news feeds (Nature, STAT News, TechCrunch), and configurable watchlists — all in one automated pipeline.

AI-Scored Findings

Every signal is scored for relevance, novelty, and strategic impact. High-priority alerts surface immediately; lower-priority items aggregate into periodic digests.

Weekly Strategic Briefings

AI-generated weekly reports synthesize all findings into actionable intelligence: competitor moves, market trends, partnership opportunities, and threat assessments.

Customizable Watch Lists

Define competitors, technologies, therapeutic areas, and keywords to monitor. AI Radar focuses on what matters to your business and filters out noise.

How It Works

1

Configure Your Radar

Define your competitive landscape — companies to track, technology keywords, therapeutic areas, geographic regions. Set alert thresholds and reporting cadence.

2

Continuous Scanning

AI agents autonomously scan all 7 sources, extract relevant signals, cross-reference findings, and score each item for strategic impact and reliability.

3

Briefings & Alerts

Receive real-time high-priority alerts and comprehensive weekly briefings. Each finding includes source links, AI analysis, and recommended actions.
