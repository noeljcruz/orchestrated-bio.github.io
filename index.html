Our Differentiators

Why Orchestrated Biosciences?

We don't just apply AI to biology — we build from the biology up, ensuring every tool we create is grounded in real science and real clinical need.

Local-First & Data Sovereign

Pharma can't send proprietary data to cloud APIs. Government agencies have sovereignty requirements. Academic institutions have IRB concerns. Our tools deploy locally — your data never leaves your environment.

HIPAA-aware Air-gap compatible On-prem / VPC

Careful Engineering, Not a Wrapper

PhD-designed agentic systems with domain-tuned orchestration, composable analysis primitives, and semantic routing that improves with use. Built by researchers who publish in the field, not prompt engineers.

Human-in-the-Loop Science

Our AI is not fully autonomous by design. It encourages data exploration, reinforces stepwise discovery, and keeps researchers in control — the way the scientific method demands.

Open Core Model

Free, self-hostable open-source core for community adoption. Managed SaaS for convenience. Enterprise licensing with private data integration, custom knowledge graphs, and priority support for pharma R&D.

Our Team

Built by Scientists & Engineers

Our founding team brings together deep biomedical research, agentic AI development, military-grade discipline, and strategic business execution.

Alex Nesta, PhD

Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Geneticist and data scientist with a PhD from UConn Health, specializing in RNA biology and cancer genomics. Pioneered research integrating long-read and short-read RNA sequencing to analyze transposable element-mediated alternative splicing in human breast cancer across six years at UConn and The Jackson Laboratory for Genomic Medicine. Subsequently spent 3.5 years as a bioinformatics consultant serving pharma and biotech — developing spatial single-cell analysis methods, discovering autoimmune biomarkers, and identifying drug response signatures across omics datasets for clients spanning large pharma to early-stage biotech and academic labs. Now channeling a decade of experience at the bench and in industry into agentic AI systems that make complex genomic analysis accessible to every researcher.
