Mouse Selector

Intelligent JAX Mouse Model Search

Find the right transgenic mouse model with natural language queries. Describe your research question — the disease model, genetic background, or phenotype you need — and Mouse Selector searches the entire JAX database, ranks results by relevance, explains why each model matches, and proactively warns about common pitfalls like strain background effects or discontinued lines.

Key Features

Natural Language Search

Describe your research needs in plain English. No need to know exact strain names, stock numbers, or JAX nomenclature — the AI understands your intent.

AI-Powered Ranking

Results ranked by scientific relevance to your query, not just keyword matches. Each result includes an AI-generated explanation of why the model fits your needs.

Proactive Warnings

Automatically flags potential issues like strain background effects, discontinued lines, breeding complications, or known phenotypic variability before you order.

Direct JAX Links

Every result links directly to the JAX strain page with stock number, genotype, and availability. Go from query to order in minutes, not hours.

How It Works

1

Describe Your Need

Type a natural language query like "tau pathology model for Alzheimer's on C57BL/6 background" or "conditional knockout for breast cancer metastasis studies."

2

AI Searches & Ranks

The AI agent queries the JAX mouse database, evaluates each strain against your research context, and returns ranked results with match explanations.

3

Review & Order

Review AI-generated match explanations, check proactive warnings, and click through directly to the JAX ordering page for your chosen strain.
